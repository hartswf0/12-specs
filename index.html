The Protocol of Capture

Speculative Physics // Left Wall Validated
Abstract: This document validates the "Protocol of Capture" speculative framework against the "Left Wall" of biophysical and economic reality. It enforces strict "Therefore" chaining: identifying the single permitted "Lie" (Axiom) and tracing its ripples through infrastructure, politics, and psychology until they fracture against the hard limits of the human user.
The Stone Thrower BYTE_01
INPUT_LAYER // THE_STONE
The "One Big Lie": The new serfdom is invisible; we toil in the fields of Data, but think we are playing.
PROCESSING_LAYER // THE_RIPPLES
RIPPLE_1_IMMEDIATE If is true, therefore movement, speech, and attention only become "value" when captured as data; therefore anonymity is treated as non-participation.
RIPPLE_2_INFRASTRUCTURE Because is true, therefore the city is built as a Capture Engine: public space requires authentication, and access to basics is bundled with biometric/behavioral updates.
RIPPLE_3_SOCIOPOLITICAL Because reallocates resources, therefore Platform Owners become Landlords and users become Tenant Farmers, paying continuous engagement for heat, water, and transit.
RIPPLE_4_PSYCHOLOGICAL Because defines survival, therefore the individual develops Performative Exhaustion: silence feels like starvation; pausing feels like erasure.
OUTPUT_LAYER // FRICTION_EXTRACTION
At the bakery, cash is refused; only Engagement Credits buy bread. The Tenant realizes they are hungry because they were not "interesting" enough today—panicky cacophony of checkout beeps, auto-ads, and shame-breath in their throat.
<Left_Wall_Check> STATUS: COMPLIANT
Economic data confirms the shift from profit-seeking to rent-seeking ("Technofeudalism") where platforms extract value from unpaid user labor [1] [2]. "Cloud Proles" are real-world analogues to the Tenant Farmer [3].
